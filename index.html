Our Story

Born from a
Deep Obsession
with Perfection.

KŌHI — Japanese for coffee — was founded in Kyoto in 2009 by master roaster Hiroshi Tanaka. After a decade studying under the world's finest roasters from Addis Ababa to Amsterdam, he returned home with one mission: to serve coffee at its absolute purest.

Every bean we source tells a story. Every roast profile is obsessed over for weeks. Every cup is a meditation in flavour.

How We Work

From Farm to Your Cup.

01
Origin Sourcing
We visit farms personally, cupping hundreds of lots to select only the top 1% of each harvest.
02
Precision Roasting
Each bean has a unique roast profile developed over weeks, roasted in small 5kg batches.
03
Quality Control
Every roast is cupped by our Q-Graders before a single gram leaves the roastery.
04
Served with Care
Brewed to order by trained baristas who understand the final cup is a year's work.
